I am a theoretical chemist turned research software engineer.

I write research software and teach programming to researchers and lead the
[CodeRefinery](https://coderefinery.org) project. I am co-creating
the weekly [Research Software Hour](https://researchsoftwarehour.github.io/) stream
and am involved in building the community of
[Nordic research software engineers](https://nordic-rse.org/).

I work at the High Performance Computing Group,
[UiT The Arctic University of Norway](https://uit.no) and
[the Norwegian e-infrastructure for research and education](https://www.nris.no).

I've also worked at the
[Laboratoire de Chimie et Physique Quantiques](http://www.lcpq.ups-tlse.fr/?lang=en) in Toulouse,
and
[PDC](https://www.pdc.kth.se)
and
[Theoretical Chemistry and Biology](https://www.theochem.kth.se) in Stockholm.
